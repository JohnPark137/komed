# Openly Available Korean Medicine Texts

Search date: 2026-03-12

## Best match

### Dongui Bogam (동의보감, 1613)
- Best public source I found for a full Korean medicine classic.
- Official web reader: <https://app.mediclassics.kr/books/동의보감>
- Official book info: <https://app.mediclassics.kr/info>
- Why it stands out: page-by-page original text plus Korean and English translation in the official reader.
- License note: the `내손안에 동의보감` app states `CC BY-NC-ND 4.0` for its content. That allows reading and limited quotation, but not republishing the full translated work in altered form.

## Best downloadable text source

### KIOM raw-text distribution
- Source: <https://info.mediclassics.kr/apps/dist-texts/index.html#/Book/8>
- Maintainer: Korean Institute of Oriental Medicine / Mediclassics.
- The page states the distributed files may be used freely for non-commercial purposes with attribution to `한의학고전DB(mediclassics.kr)`.
- This is the cleanest legal route if you want machine-readable original text rather than a browser reader.

## Search conclusion

- I did not find a clearly open-access modern all-in-one Korean medicine textbook.
- The strongest freely available materials are official digitized classics from KIOM, especially `동의보감`.
- For current clinical use, treat these as historical/traditional sources rather than up-to-date medical guidance.
